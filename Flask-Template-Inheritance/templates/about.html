Bacha Khan University is a public sector university located in Charsadda, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

It focuses on:

  • Quality education
  • Research development
  • Skill building
The university offers programs in:
  1. Computer Science
  2. Management Sciences
  3. Social Sciences
  4. Social Sciences
  5. Natural Sciences
Its goal is to prepare students for professional careers and national development.
